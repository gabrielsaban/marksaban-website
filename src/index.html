Mark Saban

Psychotherapist - Analytical Psychologist - Jungian Analyst
Registered with IAAP & UKCP

As a senior analyst, I am affiliated with the Independent Group of Analytical Psychologists.

I hold registrations as a Jungian Analyst with the United Kingdom Council for Psychotherapy and the International Association for Analytical Psychology.

After earning an M.A. at Oxford University, I spent two decades as a performer and writer in theatre, TV, and film before embarking on a career as an analytical psychologist.

I completed my PhD at the University of Essex, where I am also a lecturer in the Department of Psychosocial and Psychoanalytic Studies.

I currently provide psychotherapy services in Oxford.
