I am a senior analyst with the Independent Group of Analytical Psychologists.

I registered as a Jungian Analyst with the United Kingdom Council for Psychotherapy and the International Association for Analytical Psychology.
I gained an M.A. at Oxford University and worked for twenty years as a performer/writer in theatre, TV, and film before training as an analytical psychologist.

I completed my PhD at the University of Essex, where I also work as a lecturer in the Department of Psychosocial and Psychoanalytic studies.

I see clients in Oxford.
